A Brief History

Our Humble Beginnings

We've been around for a long time, {{ companyAge }} years to be exact. We opened our doors on September 12, 1929 in Sometown, IL and operated as a service station and lunch counter. And we sold fishing tackle and guns too.

Our Youth

In the early 60s, we dove deep into the world of commemorative firearms. By the end of the decade, we were named #1 Firearms Retailer in America. Then we traded in the Prairie State for the Tar Heel State and reopened our doors in Anywhere, NC in the 80s.

Modern Day

There have been bumps and bruises along the way, but our passion for guns has never wavered. We continue to serve gun collectors throughout the US and many foreign countries as well. We know guns, we love guns, and we pride ourselves on topping off your collection with that special piece at a fair price!!
